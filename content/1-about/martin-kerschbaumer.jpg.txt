Bio: Martin Kerschbaumer is a founding partner of Studio Mut. He spent his formative years studying product design in Florence, and then graphic design in Bolzano and Lisbon; after that working at famous design studio in Berlin. Before founding Studio Mut he taught design 101 at the faculty of Design and Art at University of Bolzano.
